Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025/01/01

At CeyBreeze Tours, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and booking services. By accessing or using our website, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Details: Name, email, and contact information.
  • Travel Details: Departure/return dates, duration, airports, hotel type, group size, and preferences.
  • Accommodation & Transport Preferences.
  • Payment Information (processed securely via third parties).
  • Authentication Data (via Firebase Authentication).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and confirm bookings.
  • Provide pricing and package details.
  • Suggest vehicles and accommodations.
  • Communicate booking updates and confirmations.
  • Comply with legal requirements.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored in Firebase Firestore and authentication handled by Firebase Authentication. We use encryption and strict access controls. We never sell your data.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share data with hotels, transport providers, payment processors, or legal authorities when necessary.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

6. Data Retention

Booking data is retained as needed for services and legal purposes. You may request deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data.
  • Request corrections or deletions.
  • Withdraw consent for communications.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new effective date.
